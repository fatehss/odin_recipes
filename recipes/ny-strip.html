Pan-Seared New York Strip Steak

Description

This recipe will show you how to make a delicious pan seared steak using a cast iron pan and an oven. It will take around 50 minutes from start to end (assuming your steak is already defrosted - I've forgotten mine in the freezer several times!)

Ingredients

  • 12 oz New York Strip
  • 1 tsp butter
  • 1 tsp salt
  • Ground pepper
  • Cooking oil spray

Steps

  1. Pre-heat oven to 500F
  2. Leave the steak at room temperature for 30 minutes
  3. Rub the steak with the salt and pepper
  4. Heat the cast iron skillet on a high heat and apply a thin coating of cooking oil spray
  5. Sear the steak on either side until golden-brown (around 90 seconds either side). Make sure to also sear the edges of the steak for around 30 seconds each using tongs.
  6. Place the steak in a foil lined tray and put the tray into the oven
  7. Leave the steak in the oven until your meat thermometer reads the inner temperature as 130F. This usually takes around 5-7 minutes depending on the thickness of your steak
  8. Take the steak out of the oven and let it rest for around 5 minutes
  9. Place the butter on the top of the steak and enjoy!
